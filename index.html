WHO WE ARE

Our Vision, Values and Motto

We are a group of working professionals from the UK who are passionate about helping people in need around the world. Over the last 15 years we have individually and collectively carried out extensive humanitarian work with numerous charities around the world – in Syria, Palestine, Pakistan, Bangladesh and the UK. We have seen, first hand, the catastrophic effect of conflict and natural disasters on the most vulnerable people.
